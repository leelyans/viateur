Our services

Detailed List of our Services

Physiotherapy Consultations

We take the time to listen to your concerns, thoroughly assess your condition, and discuss your medical requirements.

Home Based Visits

We bring quality care to your doorstep, ensuring you receive treatment you deserve in your home.

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Recovering from fractures, joint replacements, ligament or tendon injuries, and sprains/strains.

Neurologic Rehabilitation

Supervised program designed for people with diseases, injury, or disorders of the nervous system.

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Treating conditions such as low back pain, neck pain, shoulder impingement, tennis elbow, and osteoarthritis.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Managing and rehabilitating sports-related injuries like sprained ankles, muscle strains, ACL tears, tennis/golfer's elbow, and concussions.

Geriatric Rehabilitation

Improving balance, strength, and mobility in older adults with osteoporosis, arthritis, balance disorders, age-related muscle weakness, and joint replacements.

Cardiorespiratory Rehabilitation

Tailored programs for individuals with conditions like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), asthma, post-cardiac surgery rehabilitation, heart failure, and pulmonary fibrosis.

Pain Management

Alleviating musculoskeletal pain and chronic pain syndromes such as low back pain, fibromyalgia, chronic headaches, myofascial pain syndrome, and rheumatoid arthritis.

Pediatric Rehabilitation:

Providing specialized care for children with conditions like cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, developmental delays, neuromuscular disorders, spina bifida, and torticollis (twisted neck).

Cancer Rehabilitation

We work closely with you to create a personalized rehabilitation plan. From managing treatment side effects to rebuilding physical strength and emotional resilience.

Massage Therapy

Masage therapists will customize each session to address your specific needs and provide you with a personalized journey to relaxation and rejuvenation.
