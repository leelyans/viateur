ABOUT

Viateur Physiotherapist.

Viateur NSHIMYUMUREMYI is a licensed Physiotherapist based in Kigali, Rwanda. He graduated from the University of Rwanda, College of Medicine and Health Sciences and is a member of the Rwanda Physical Therapy Organisation. With extensive experience in various settings including Orthopedic Specialized Hospitals, rehabilitation centers, and clinics, Viateur is well-equipped to accommodate the diverse needs of his clients.

Viateur holds advanced training in Orthopedics and Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation from the University of Rwanda, College of Medicine and Health Sciences. This specialized training enables him to provide comprehensive care for Orthopedic conditions and deliver effective rehabilitation interventions for individuals with musculoskeletal injuries or disorders. His expertise in Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation allows him to address the specific needs of patients with cardiovascular and respiratory conditions, providing tailored treatment plans for post-surgical recovery, cardiac rehabilitation, and pulmonary rehabilitation.

He specializes in stroke rehabilitation and therapeutic exercise approaches to orthopedic problems of the extremities. Viateur has received training in Basic and Advanced non-Surgical Club Foot Treatment using the Ponseti Method from Centura Health. Additionally, he has undergone training in Sports Medicine by the African Union of Sports Medicine and the French Anti-doping Agency.

In his clinical practice, Viateur empowers his patients to play an active role in their recovery and guides them towards achieving their goals. He believes in an evidence-based approach to assessment and treatment, leveraging the power of communication to help patients take ownership of their healing process. With his diverse training and expertise, Viateur NSHIMYUMUREMYI ensures that his patients receive high-quality, individualized Physiotherapy services to facilitate their rehabilitation journey.
